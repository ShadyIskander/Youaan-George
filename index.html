My Story

📷 Photo coming soon

My name is Youaan Saleh, and I am from Egypt. I am actively serving in ministry through worship, music, and youth work.

For many years, I have been involved in worship ministry as a musician, worship leader, sound engineer, and volunteer in different churches, conferences, camps, and events. Music has always been one of the main ways God has allowed me to serve others and point people toward Him. I have a deep passion for worship and for creating spaces where people can encounter God's presence.

Over the past months, God has been placing the DTS on my heart in a very clear and consistent way. As I prayed about my next steps, I felt Him inviting me to grow deeper in my relationship with Him, strengthen my biblical foundation, and be equipped for greater ministry. The opportunity to learn more about worship, music, songwriting, and worship leadership is especially meaningful to me because these are areas where I feel called to serve.

I believe this DTS will not only help me grow personally and spiritually, but it will also equip me to better serve my church, worship teams, and the people God has entrusted to me. My desire is to return with a deeper understanding of God's heart, stronger leadership skills, and practical tools that will help me lead others into authentic worship and discipleship.

For me, DTS is much more than a school. It is an opportunity to intentionally set aside time to know God more deeply, grow in my faith, and be shaped by Him for whatever He has next. I am excited to see how God will use this season to transform my life and prepare me for the calling He has placed before me.

Why DTS?

DTS is a 6-month program. The first 3 months are intensive teaching on faith, identity, and calling. The final 3 months are outreach anywhere in the world. It is not just a trip; it is a transformation. I believe God is calling me to this season, and I need your help to make it happen.
